What is El Niño?

El Niño is a warming of the central and eastern tropical Pacific Ocean. It shifts rainfall patterns, monsoons, and seasonal temperature signals.

What to watch

  • Niño 3.4 sea-surface anomalies.
  • Trade wind and jet stream changes.
  • Local forecasts for day-to-day decisions.
