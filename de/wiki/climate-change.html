2026 Climate Outlook

Long-term climate signals show warming, more intense rainfall in some regions, and deeper droughts in others. For travelers and planners, the key is to combine long-term awareness with daily local forecasts.

What the data shows

  • Global average temperatures continue to rise relative to the 20th-century baseline.
  • Heat waves are becoming more frequent in many mid-latitude cities.
  • Extreme precipitation events are intensifying where moisture is available.

Practical tips

  • Check forecasts close to departure, not just seasonal averages.
  • Pack for temperature swings, especially in continental climates.
  • Follow official warnings during severe weather rather than relying on general trends.
