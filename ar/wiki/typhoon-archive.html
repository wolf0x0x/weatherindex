Major Typhoon Archive

How major typhoon records are reviewed and compared across seasons.

Historical typhoon archives help compare landfall risk, rainfall extremes, surge damage, and recovery lessons.

How to read an archive

  • Track intensity together with landfall timing and population exposure.
  • Separate meteorological strength from social impact.
  • Use archives for planning, not for assuming a future storm will repeat exactly.
